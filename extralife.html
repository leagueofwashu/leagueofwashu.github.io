Extra Life is Back!

League of WashU is excited to be hosting an Extra Life event again in the DUC on November 7th, 2015 for 12 hours. For 2014, over 100 fellow WashU students raised an incredible $6000 for the Children's Miracle Network. We didn't expect such a resounding success but you, our fellow students, stepped up to the occasion and did something truly amazing.

But that was 2014, this is 2015, and we want it to be bigger and better. For the kids. Of course, we can't do this alone, which is where you come in. Join us and you can help make a serious difference in the world for the people that need it most.

So click the "JOIN TEAM" button next to our logo, create your profile, and start raising funds for the children. Tell your friends, tell your families, tell them about the great cause you are working towards. Any donation or awareness helps.

Kids are calling to us for help, will you answer them?

FAQ

Is there a fundraising requirement?
$10 or 10 emails (please send your emails via Extra Life) is the minimum required to celebrate with us.
Can I bring my own game?
Absolutely and in fact, we encourage you to bring your own game.
What games will be at Extra Life?
For starters, there will be League of Legends, Smash Bros, Dance Dance Revolution, Magic the Gathering, Hearthstone, Counter-Strike GO, various board games, Wii and XBox games at the DUC and many, many more.
Is Extra Life a full day commitment? Do I have to be there for all twelve hours?
Absolutely not! You can come and go as you please.
Does Extra Life count towards APO service hour requirements?
Yes it does!
Will there be refreshments?
Of course :)
