Riot Q&A

Ever wonder what it's like to work at Riot? Then come out to League of WashU's Riot Q&A, your chance to meet with Riot, ask questions, and get the answers you've been dying to know! All panelists are WashU alumni working at Riot's St. Louis Office and most importantly, free Chipotle will be at the event.
