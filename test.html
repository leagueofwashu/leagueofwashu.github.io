League of WashU about us

League of WashU is the premier source of League of Legends and eSports culture and entertainment on campus. As a club, we seek to bring together students with a common interest in not only League of Legends, but eSports as a whole. Our goal is to create and organize fun and engaging events as well as creating a community within the student body.

Events what we do

From 24-hour charity gaming marathons to Riot Q&As and from Summoner School to WashU's own LCS-style tournament, we promise that there's something for everyone. So no matter if you just started playing video games yesterday or if you've been Diamond I for three seasons now, come to our events to learn about eSports, make friends, and have fun. Check out our schedule for more details!
